ear seeds

non-invasive (needless)

and pretty much trouble-free

Ear seeds are tiny Vaccaria seeds or little stainless-steel pellets stuck on your ears with clear adhesive tape. If they're still hanging on, gently peel them off yourself. Ear seed therapy works best over time—the gains stack.
